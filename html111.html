ICC MENS T20 WORLD CUP 2024

The 2024 ICC Men's T20 World Cup was the ninth edition of the tournament, co-hosted by Cricket West Indies and USA Cricket from 1 to 29 June 2024, marking the first major ICC tournament to include matches played in the United States. A total of twenty teams competed in 55 matches across six venues in the West Indies and three in the United States, with a total attendance of 190,000 at the U.S. venues. India emerged as champions, winning their second T20 World Cup title by defeating South Africa by seven runs in the final at Kensington Oval in Bridgetown, Barbados on 29 June 2024. India became the first team to win the T20 World Cup while remaining undefeated throughout the tournament, winning all eight of their matches.
